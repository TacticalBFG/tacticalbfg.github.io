What's inside

Built for precision.

Every feature in Helicity is designed to be lightweight, instant, and unobtrusive — just the tools you need, nothing you don't.

⏸
Timer Control
Pause and resume the in-game clock at will. Analyze the board without the pressure of the ticking timer.
💣
Mine ESP
Overlay highlights every hidden mine in real-time. Study patterns, learn layouts, and accelerate your skill curve.
🔊
Sound FX
Trigger the classic explosion sound on demand. Useful for stream highlights or just having fun with your board.
🪟
Native UI
Built on Neutralino — no Electron bloat. A slim, GPU-light overlay that sits perfectly on top of your game.
